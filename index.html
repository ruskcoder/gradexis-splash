Safe and Secure

Your privacy is our top priority and we would like to be open with what is collected. All your grade data is encrypted with HTTPS and securely transmitted from Gradexis servers and is never stored. We only fetch the information you request.

Data Collected:

  • Your Username
  • School Name

We use this information for referrals and student distribution monitoring. It is securely stored, and no individual data is shared with third parties.
