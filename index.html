Gradexis

Academic Success at your Fingertips.

Gradexis is a simple grade and GPA calculator for all students in KatyISD and more! Easily calculate your grades and future predictions in just two clicks!

Features

Grade Calculator

We can calculate and predict your grades for the future easily in just a few clicks! Have perfect and accurate representations of your grades.

Linked Todo List

Using a special system, we are able to link your todo list with Google Tasks, Microsoft Todo, and even Canvas!

Data Fetching

Our calculator uses a neat API and data fetching system to get your details and apply them instantly!
