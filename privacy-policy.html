Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12/27/2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy was created by @ruskcoder. This app is intended for use by users, and this document explains how data is collected, used, and stored.

Information Collected

Login Details

The app requires login details such as username and password. These credentials are used only for authentication purposes and are not stored anywhere after being used for login.

Personal Identifying Information

The app collects username and school only. This information is used for the referral system and statistical and performance monitoring purposes, such as understanding how large and geographically spread out the user base is.

Security

All information transmitted between the app and the server uses HTTPS encryption. Identifying data is stored in a secure database to protect against unauthorized access.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or rent any personal identifying information to third parties. Data is used solely for the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy.

Links to Other Sites

This app may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites. Users are encouraged to review the privacy policies of any external websites they visit.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here.
